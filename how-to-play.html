Start in the corners

You are black in the bottom-left. Your opponent is purple in the top-right.

2

Pick a legal color

You cannot pick your color or your opponent's color. First move: no black, no purple.

Red Green Yellow Blue Black Purple
3

Your territory changes

If you choose red, every square you own turns red.

->
4

Capture touching matches

You take matching squares that touch your area up, down, left, or right.

Side touches count. Diagonals do not.

5

Take turns until full

Opponent moves next. Keep choosing colors until every square is owned.

29You 27Opponent

Rules at a glance

  • Owned squares always become the color you choose.
  • You capture connected squares of that same color.
  • Opponent-owned squares are blocked.
  • Most owned squares at the end wins.
