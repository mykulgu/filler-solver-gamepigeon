How to Play Filler on GamePigeon

Solver How to Play

Goal: own more squares than your opponent when the board is full.

8 x 7 board 6 colors Random start colors Either player may start No diagonal captures
1

Start in the corners

You are in the bottom-left. Your opponent is in the top-right. The starting colors can change.

2

Pick a legal color

The player moving cannot pick either player's current color.

Red Yellow Purple Black Blue Green
3

Territory changes color

Every square the moving player owns becomes the color they choose.

->
4

Capture touching matches

The moving player takes matching squares that touch their area up, down, left, or right.

Side touches count. Diagonals do not.

5

Take turns until full

Players alternate moves until every square is owned.

29You 27Opponent

Rules at a glance

  • Owned squares always become the color you choose.
  • You capture connected squares of that same color.
  • Opponent-owned squares are blocked.
  • Most owned squares at the end wins.
